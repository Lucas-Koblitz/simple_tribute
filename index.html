How he became a hero to Fluminense fans

2008

Fluminense Football Club gets to the Libertadores Cup final for the first time in the club history. Fluminense won the second leg with a hat-trick of their top scorer. But even so, the brazziliand team lost in the penalty shootout in Rio de Janeiro, leaving the local fans with a trauma that was only solved 15 years later, by a 21 years old boy.


2023, the begining

2023 Death Matches of Libertadores Cup

John Kennedy scored at least a gol in each round of the competition. An impressive feat to a 21 year old who doesn't feel nervous at all, even when he's being watched by thousands of people.

The final of this year sounds like a script taken directly from a sports movie. A movie where the protagonist is John Kennedy. Because in a year filled with difficulties, he gets to play for 20 minutes, wich were enough for him. Enough to turn a tense draw against Boca Juniors in a historical win that took the sadness of 2008 out of Fluminense fan's heart, the first continental cup of Fluminense.
